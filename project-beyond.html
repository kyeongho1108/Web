Beyond the Glass (2026)
Designed by Kyeongho Park / Wonderlab KAIST

Beyond the Glass is an XR interface for museums that lets visitors explore exhibits and interact with objects displayed inside glass cases.

Three types of interfaces for interacting with museum exhibits (Bare Hands, Haptic Wand, Video See-through).

Designed head-tracking interactions to improve transitions and depth, creating a more immersive spatial experience.

Depth cue design for improved spatial awareness.
