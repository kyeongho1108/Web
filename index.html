Kyeongho Park is a designer based in Seoul whose work spans from traditional industrial design to systemic design innovation.

His work explores how design can contribute to a better future in practical and sustainable ways. His current interest lies in design that can sustain itself, design that goes beyond the lifespan of a single product and becomes part of the foundation of everyday life.
