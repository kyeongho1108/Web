The Bell (2026)
Designed by Kyeongho Park

Neighborhood connections in Korea are fading. From apartments to small villas, closed front doors often feel like closed hearts. What if a small window on the doorstep could help people connect again? Could a doorbell become a way to open hearts, not just doors?

In the past, people would hang portraits of those who represented the household.
At the entrance, we hang things that protect and conceal us.
But these days, in our homes, we often hang things that reflect what we love.
